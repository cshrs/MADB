Introduction to Digital Literacy: Resources and Guide

About the Website

This website serves to help aid and improve your digital literacy and assist in being safe online. To navigate the website you can use your keyboard (left and right) to navigate through the pages. Feel free to access the accessibility buttons to enlarge and shrink text, as well as read aloud.

The website is designed with wide-screen landscape use in mind i.e computer screens and tablets, if you are viewing using a smartphone change device if possible.

This project was created in part of MAD(B) 2454797 at the University of Glasgow.
