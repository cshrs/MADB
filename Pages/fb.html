Introduction to Digital Literacy: Resources and Guide

Introduction

Privacy settings on Facebook help you control who can see your content and personal information. This tutorial guides you through adjusting these settings for enhanced privacy.

Steps to Change Your Privacy Settings

  1. Log In to Your Facebook Account: Visit the Facebook website and log in with your credentials.
  2. Access Settings & Privacy: Click on the downward arrow at the top right corner of your homepage and select 'Settings & Privacy' > 'Settings'.
  3. Navigate to Privacy Settings: On the left-hand side menu, click 'Privacy'. Here you will find various settings related to who can see your posts, how people can find you on Facebook, and how you receive friend requests.
  4. Edit Your Privacy Settings: Click 'Edit' next to any setting you wish to change. For example, to adjust who can see your future posts, click 'Edit' next to 'Your activity' and select your preferred audience from the dropdown menu.
  5. Review Other Privacy Options: Consider reviewing other sections such as 'How People Find and Contact You' to adjust settings like who can send you friend requests or look you up using your email address or phone number.

Conclusion

By customizing your privacy settings on Facebook, you take important steps towards controlling your online presence and protecting your personal information. Regularly review these settings to ensure they align with your privacy preferences.
