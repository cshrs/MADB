8. What is a password manager?

9. What is two-factor authentication (2FA)?

A method of securing accounts by using two different passwords
An additional layer of security that requires something you know and something you have
A way to reset your password if you forget it

10. What is the purpose of a firewall?

To protect your computer from physical damage
To filter incoming and outgoing network traffic to prevent unauthorized access
